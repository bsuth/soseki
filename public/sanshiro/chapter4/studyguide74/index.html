Sanshir≈ç went to the kitchen to put fresh water in his bucket. Mineko took the duster and broom and headed upstairs.

"Come up," she called to Sanshir≈ç from above.

"What is it?" Sanshir≈ç appeared at the bottom of the stairs with his bucket. She was standing up on the dark landing. All he could see was the bright white of her apron. "What is it?"

"It's too dark. I can't go up."

"Why not?"

"I just can't."

Sanshir≈ç saw no reason to press the matter further. He slipped past her and stepped up onto the floor. He set his bucket down in the dark balcony corridor and tried to open a storm shutter. He couldn't find the bolt to unlatch it. Mineko came up after him.

"Won't open yet?" She went to the opposite side. "Here it is."

Without speaking, Sanshir≈ç moved toward her. As their hands were almost touching, he stumbled over his bucket and kicked up a racket. When they finally succeeded in opening the shutter, bright sunlight flooded the room. It was almost blinding. They looked at each other and couldn't refrain from laughing.
