<h3 className="section">Rough translation</h3>
<p>"However, one can't be too cautious with women these days. Things aren't the way they used to be. One must watch out."</p>
<p>"What do you mean? Do you think my wife's taken a secret lover in Tōkyō?"</p> 
<p>"No. I'm sure your wife is trustworthy."</p>
<p>"I'm relieved to hear that. Then what is it one must watch out for."</p>
<p>"Your wife may be true. Your wife may be true, but ..."</p>
<p>"Who is it that can't be trusted?"</p>
<p>"There are many hereabouts. Perhaps you've heard about the Tōyama girl?"</p>
<p>"No, I haven't heard."</p>
<p>"You haven't heard? She's the foremost beauty in these parts. She's of such beauty that the teachers at the school all call her Madonna. You're not aware?"</p>
<p>"Oh, Madonna? I thought it must be the name of some geisha."</p>
<p>"Not at all. Madonna is a foreign word, and it means a woman of great beauty."</p>
<p>"Really. That's news to me."</p>
<p>"Most likely that name came from the art teacher."</p>
<p>"From Noda, you mean?"</p>
<p>"No, it was Yoshikawa."</p>
<p>"And this Madonna is untrustworthy?"</p>
<p>"This Madonna is an untrustworthy Madonna."</p>
<p>"What a bother. Women with nicknames have never been respectable, so maybe the same goes for Madonna."</p>
<p>"That's the truth. Remember Kijin no Omatsu and Dakki no Ohyaku. Frightful women they were."</p>
<p>"Do you suppose Madonna is that same type of woman?"</p>
<p>"Let me tell you. That gentleman Koga, who brought you to us. Well, she was promised to him as his bride."</p>
<p>"Incredible. I never took Uranari for a ladies' man. One can't judge by appearance. I'll have to be a bit more careful."</p>
