<p>As it touched the neck, off went the head!</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whir―whir―whir―whir!</p>

<p>Quietly waiting the fatal shock;</p>
<p>The axe it severed it right in twain,</p>

<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whir―whir―whir―whir!</p>
<p>Salisbury's countess, she would not die</p>
<p>As a proud dame should―decorously.</p>
<p>Lifting my axe, I split her skull,</p>
<p>And the edge since then has been notched and dull.</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Whir―whir―whir―whir!</p>
<p>Queen Catherine Howard gave me a fee, ―</p>
<p>A chain of gold―to die easily:</p>
<p>And her costly present she did not rue,</p>
<p>For I touched her head, and away it flew!</p>
