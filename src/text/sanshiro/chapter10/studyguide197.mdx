<h3 class="section">Rough translation</h3>
<p>She looked up at him as before. Her hands made no motion to accept. She didn't stir in the least. Her expression remained subdued. He wasn't quite sure what her answer had implied.</p>
<p>"Just a bit more. Shall we?" A voice sounded from behind. Haraguchi had turned and was looking their way. Brush between his fingers, he smiled as he tugged at the tip of his beard. Mineko, still seated, braced both arms on the armrests to force herself upright.</p>
<p>"Will it be much longer?" Sanshirō asked her in a quiet voice.</p>
<p>"About an hour," Mineko answered him, also in a quiet voice.</p>
<p>Sanshirō went back to the round table. Mineko resumed her pose. Haraguchi, smoking his pipe again, began to work his brush.</p>
<p>"Ogawa-san, take a look at Satomi-san's eyes," Haraguchi said with his back turned.</p>
<p>Sanshirō looked as requested. Mineko lowered her fan and relaxed her pose. She turned sideways to looked through the window at the garden.</p>
<p>"No, don't turn sideways. Just when I'd started painting."</p>
<p>"Then what's the talk for?" She turned back to face them again.</p>
<p>Haraguchi explained himself. "I didn't mean to throw you off. I want to consult with Ogawa-san."</p>
<p>"On what?"</p>
<p>"Resume your pose, and I'll explain. That's it. Elbows a little more forward. Okay, Ogawa-san, look at the eyes I've drawn. Have I captured the expression of my model?"</p>
