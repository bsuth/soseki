<h3 class="section">Rough translation</h3>
<p>"Have Nonomiya-san and Satomi-san know each other for long?"</p> 
<p>"Yes. They're friends."</p>
<p>Sanshirō wondered about the nature of their friendship. Just a casual male-female friendship seemed unlikely. However, he had no way to probe this further.</p>
<p>"I hear that Professor Hirota taught Nonomiya-san."</p>
<p>"Yes."</p>
<p>Her short affirmations impeded the flow of conversation.</p>
<p>"Do you feel about lodging at the Satomi place?"</p>
<p>"Me? I suppose I wouldn't mind it. But I hate to impose on Mineko's older brother."</p>
<p>"She has an older brother?"</p>
<p>"Yes. He graduated the same year as my brother."</p>
<p>"Then he's also a scientist?"</p>
<p>"No, he graduated from the college of law. There was also an eldest brother who was a friend of Professor Hirota, but he passed away at an early age, so now there's only Kyōsuke."</p>
<p>"And their parents?"</p>
<p>"They have none." Yoshiko laughed lightly, as though the idea of Mineko having parents was somehow absurd. Apparently they'd passed away years before, and Yoshiko had no memory of them.</p>
<p>"That's how Mineko is acquainted with Professor Hirota then?"</p>
<p>"Yes. They say that her eldest brother was very close to the professor. And Mineko is interested in English, so she sometimes goes to him for lessons."</p>
<p>"Does Satomi-san visit you here?"</p>
<p>Without his noticing, Yoshiko had resumed her watercolor work. She seemed fully comfortable with his presence, and she was more talkative when painting.</p>
<p>"Mineko?" While asking, she shaded in the thatched roof below the persimmon tree. "It's a little dark, don't you think?" She held it out for him to see.</p>
<p>"Yes, it's too dark." This time Sanshirō answered her honestly.</p>
<p>Yoshiko wetted her brush and dabbed the dark area. "She does come over." She finally returned to Sanshirō's question.</p>
<p>"Often?"</p>
<p>"Fairly often." Yoshiko's attention was back on her drawing paper. Since she'd resumed her painting, Sanshirō found the pace of their conversation comfortable.</p>
